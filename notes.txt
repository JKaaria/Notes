**Title:**
Enhancing Trade Matching Efficiency

**Problem Statement:**
- Manual processes and reliance on client emails for trade mismatch resolution.
- Inefficiencies, delays, and errors in the fixed income middle office.

**Benefits:**
- Automated data preparation with Alteryx for streamlined processing.
- Efficient Bloomberg integration via Excel macros for API calls and email generation.
- Reduced manual efforts, improving overall efficiency.
- Prompt communication to trade support teams for quick resolution.

**Impact if Not Done:**
- Increased operational risks and potential financial losses.
- Delayed trade resolutions impacting timely processing.
- Client dissatisfaction due to slow response times.
- Inefficient resource utilization affecting productivity.

**Objective:**
Mitigate challenges, streamline trade matching, and enhance operational efficiency in the fixed income middle office.
